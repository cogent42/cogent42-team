Your team's brain, one Telegram bot per teammate.

Each member gets a personal AI agent on Telegram, backed by a shared knowledge base. Decisions, projects, server info, and workflows are auto-extracted from chat and sent emails. Private preferences and personal notes never leave your bot.

Get set up (for new users)

1.
Create a Telegram bot. DM @BotFather on Telegram, run /newbot, pick a name and username (must end in bot). BotFather will hand you an HTTP API token — copy it.
2.
Find your numeric Telegram user ID. DM @userinfobot — it'll reply with your numeric ID (like 390910591).
3.
Send your admin three things: your work email, your numeric Telegram ID, and the bot token from BotFather. Your admin will provision your bot in this control plane — it spins up a dedicated container scoped to your account.
4.
Once provisioned, DM your bot. Anything you say goes through Claude with the team's shared knowledge as context. Conversations are auto-extracted into knowledge entries after 60 seconds of idle, or immediately on /done.
5.
Connect your Gmail (optional). Sent emails get extracted into the team brain. DM your bot /gmail for a one-click consent link, or ask your admin to mint one for you. The flow grants read-only access; we never touch your inbox unless you opt in.

Slash commands you can DM your bot

/done flush this conversation for extraction
/recent last 10 facts saved for you
/forget <text> delete facts matching the text
/private <id> flip a fact to private (use prefix from /recent)
/gmail connect your Gmail (read-only, sent only)
/help list these commands

Privacy contract

Facts in the categories preference, rule, and personal are always private — visible only to you. Everything else (project, decision, server, config, workflow, bug, mistake) defaults to team-visible. You can flip any fact to private with /private <id>, and disable team-sharing entirely from the admin UI.

Need help?

Reach out to your administrator at tech@katha.today for provisioning, Gmail consent links, or anything that needs admin access.
